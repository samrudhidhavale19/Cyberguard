Built-in Scam Rules

Urgent Requests

Messages that pressure you to act urgently without verification are often phishing attempts.

Account Access Claims

If someone asks for credentials, passwords, or OTP codes, treat it as scam content.

Financial Incentives

Unexpected offers of money, prizes, or refunds should be verified independently.

Suspicious Links

Check URLs carefully. Shortened or obfuscated links are common tools for redirecting victims.
