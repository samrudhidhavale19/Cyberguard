About CyberGuard

CyberGuard is a beginner-friendly project designed to help people identify online scams like phishing, fake messages, fraudulent links, and social engineering attempts.

What this tool does

  • Message scanning: analyze text, detect suspicious words, phrases, and patterns.
  • Confidence signal: categorize text as safe, warning, or scam and show an estimated confidence.
  • Report system: submit scam examples using the Live Feed page to help build awareness and optionally train your own analysis data.
  • Security guidance: includes tips for recognizing scams and acting safely.
  • Privacy focus: all analysis is done locally or via API; no permanent storage of your text.
  • Educational resource: learn about common scam tactics and how to protect yourself online.
